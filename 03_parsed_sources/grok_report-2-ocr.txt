This RAND-inspired system combines duplex-numeric fixed placement with content-
driven branching and a mandatory register for flexibility. It avoids rigid topical hierarchies
(per historical RAND/Luhmann principles) while supporting modern online distribution,
multimedia documents, and scalable sorting. The multi-layer design ensures

maintainability: storage is decoupled from logic, which is decoupled from UI.
